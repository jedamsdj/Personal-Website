About Me

Engineer, Developer, Designer

Devon Jedamski is an engineer, developer, and maker. He is a conceptual design engineer at Northrop Grumman in Los Angeles, California. Devon has experience in air breathing propulsion, software development, smart grid design, and renewable energy.
